About the game

Meet the protagonist of the game - Arway.

Like any modern man, Arway spends a lot of time on social networking.

Arway met his sweetheart on the Internet, and live communication they both prefer virtual. But just today he decides to make her an offer. And how? Through The Internet. But the most important point drop server. Unable to stand the suspense, he takes off and runs to his beloved.

Game action begins in the office from which he escapes. At the end of the level Arway meets his boss who is very unhappy with his escape. The next level - metropolitan streets, Arway neyubhodimo to get home to his beloved. And Arway in the house of his beloved, but he has yet to find her. At the end of the game Arway know whether she will say yes to him. The answer will depend on how you pass the game!
